⚠ Missing: Caching Layer
Add Redis/Memcached between your API servers and DB to reduce read latency. Your tweet timeline reads will cause N+1 DB queries without caching.
✕ SPOF: Single Database
Your database has no read replicas. Add at least 2 read replicas and a primary for writes. Use primary-replica replication.
💡 Consider: Fan-out Strategy
For Twitter at scale, use Fan-out-on-write for users with <10k followers and Fan-out-on-read for celebrities (Hybrid approach).
✓ Good: Load Balancer
Good inclusion of load balancer. Consider specifying the algorithm (Round Robin, Least Connections).
